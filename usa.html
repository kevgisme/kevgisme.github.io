USA Bike Trip

From Montana to Phoenix

1st June - 20th June 2015

Kevin Gill

Cycling 1300 miles in 19 days, sleeping in a hammock

Wanting to spend my time off in the summer doing something active I decided to challenge myself with something out of the ordinary. Having previously only cycled a maximum of around 20 miles in a day, this trip would take me our of my comfort zone, cycling 70-80 miles a day with no booked accomodation.

I bit the bullet after some consideration of the madness of the trip and booked a flight from London to Bozeman, Montana, and a return flight from Phoenix, Arizona to London 19 days later. The plan was to sleep primarily in my hammock, keeping accomodation costs down but also making the trip that much more difficult without the comfort of a bed waiting for me at the end of the long days on the bike.

After landing in Bozeman at 8:40pm, after an 18 hour journey with 3 flights, I began searching for my hotel

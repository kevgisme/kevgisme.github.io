Yellowstone National Park

2nd June

Kevin Gill

An unbelievable day of cycling

Uphill most of the morning into Yellowstone, by early afternoon I started to come face to face with the amazing scenery and wildlife that the biggest National Park in North America has to offer. Lots of hills top conquer, but the uphill slog of the previous day all seemed worth it for the many rewards including; Geizers, Hot Springs, Bison, Grizzly Bear (from a distance!), and breathtaking landscape throughout.
